Kidney Health Tips

9 Ways to Keep Your Kidneys Healthy

Here are highlighted tips for maintaining kidney health:

  1. Stay hydrated
  2. Limit NSAIDs
  3. Control blood pressure and sugar
  4. Exercise regularly
  5. Quit smoking

Remember Health Is Wealth

Foods That Support Kidney Health

Feel free to explore a variety of foods that promote kidney health.

  • Low-potassium fruits (e.g., apples, berries)
  • Whole vegetables (e.g., carrots, bell peppers)
  • Moderate-protein sources (e.g., chicken, fish)
  • Healthy oils (e.g., olive oil, avocado)
  • Adequate hydration (e.g., water, herbal teas)

Avoid excess sodium and ultra-processed foods.

Expert Insights & Guidelines

8 Golden Rules for Kidney Health

Ways of approaching kidney health include:

  1. Understand your kidneys
  2. Monitor blood pressure and diabetes
  3. Get regular tests
  4. Focus on long-term quality of life with your nephrologist

Understanding the Renal Diet

  • Low sodium
  • Low phosphorus
  • Low potassium
  • Controlled protein

A renal diet reduces sodium, phosphorus, potassium, and protein when needed—supporting kidneys while lowering cardiovascular risks.

Why We Care

Millions worldwide live with kidney disease. Prevention through diet, testing, and lifestyle is our mission.
